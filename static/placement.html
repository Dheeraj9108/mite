Employability Skills Development Program

MITE has been conducting various Programs on Soft Skills, Life Skills for all its students under the Employability Skills Development Program (ESDP). The ESDP Cell was launched in the Year 2010, with the objective of making a student ‘Industry Ready’ and bridge the gap between classroom teaching and Industry needs. The ESDP Cell regularly conducts various programs which are part of their weekly schedule. The Program exposes the students to the various requirements of the ‘Interview Preparedness’. A team of dedicated faculty exclusively for ESDP looks after the Programs offered under the same.
