INSTITUTE STRATEGIC PLAN
Strategic Plan

Mangalore Institute of Technology & Engineering established with a vision of imparting globally competitive quality education is the most admired and preferred engineering institution in Coastal Karnataka. To attain excellence, it is desirable to develop Strategic Plans which is a continuous process with a specific focus on achieving Short term and Long term plans which enhance the existing capacities of the institution to become dynamic, demand-driven, quality conscious and efficient.


Recognizing the Vision, Mission and core values of the institute, analysing current and expected future scenarios, with SWOC analysis of the institution, Long Term Plans (2012-2020) and Short Term Plans (2012-2016) and (2016-2020) are developed through the inputs from all the stake holders. The final draft is presented to Governing Council for detailed review and discussion, the suggestions are incorporated towards its effective implementation.

- Long Term Plans : (2012-2020)
  • LTP1: To attain NBA accreditation for all eligible UG programs and NAAC accreditation for the institution.
  • LTP2: To establish an Incubation Centre to facilitate entrepreneurship and create Centres of Excellence through reputed global industry / University collaborations.
+ Short term plan - 2012 2016
+ Short term plan - 2016 2020

Strategic Plan (2020- 2028)

Mangalore Institute of Technology & Engineering started with a vision of imparting globally competitive quality education is setting a new benchmark every year with incredible milestones, has earned a brand name and positions itself as most admired and preferred Engineering institution in Coastal Karnataka. Having achieved most of the set targets with marked success Strategic Plans for the next phase with a specific focus on achieving Short term and Long term plans has been developed to attain next level of excellence.


Recognizing the Vision, Mission and core values of the institute, analysing current and expected future scenarios with SWOC analysis of the institution and meticulous review of the accomplishments of strategic plans of previous phase, the Short Term Plans (2020-2024) and Long Term Plans (2020-2028) are developed through the inputs from all the stakeholders. The final draft is presented to Governing council for detailed review and discussion, the suggestions are incorporated towards its effective implementation.

- Long Term Plans : (2020-28)
  • LTP1: To create resources/facilities to attain the status of degree-awarding institution under National Education Policy 2020
  • LTP2: To create Centres of Excellence by utilizing the resources and expertise in each domain.
