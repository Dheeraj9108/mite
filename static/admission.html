ADMISSION FOR MBA
Download Brochure

MITE offers MBA course that helps in developing a competent cadre of business executives to meet the country’s growing requirements in the field of Business Management.

The Course is spread over four semesters with the following Dual specialization in the Management sector.

– Marketing & Finance

– Marketing & Human Resource Management

Eligibility:

Admissions to the first year of the MBA course will be open to students who have passed the University graduation examination conducted by any University or any other examinations recognised by the University or equivalent thereto, with a minimum of 50% aggregate in arts , commerce or science.

Selection of the candidates will be made on the basis of rank obtained in the entrance test.

ENTRANCE TEST

Admission to MBA program is done on the basis of marks obtained in any one of the recognised entrance tests like PGCET( Karnataka), MAT, KMAT, CAT etc.

Global Exposure : MBA Students of MITE can get additional Global exposure to undergo 10 days of special training in a leading Business School in Singapore, as an optional part of the course.
