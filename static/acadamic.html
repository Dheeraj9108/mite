Academic Excellence

MITE believes in providing the best education to students and enable them to achieve an exuberant result in their lives. The institution is working on a multi-pronged strategy to achieve this goal. The Institution’s multiple efforts to shape skill and competence in students has resulted in excellent results year after year. Highly qualified teaching faculty with vast experience, industrial flair and great passion for teaching aids in inculcating right attitude and skill among the students. Interaction with industry and national and International universities of repute brings experiences to the classroom which limits education not to just theory at MITE. Unique focussed mentoring system shapes students in the right direction. The proof of the pudding is in the results.
