Dr. M S
Ganesha Prasad
Principal

Dr. M S Ganesha Prasad , a highly qualified technocrat with 30 years of Teaching, Research, and Administrative experience is the Principal of MITE. Dr. Ganesha Prasad has received his Master’s Degree from Kuvempu University, and Ph.D. from the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. He is a Member of the Indian Society for Technical Education, Institute of Engineers – Kolkatta, and Indian Institute of Production Engineers.




Dr. M S Ganesha Prasad has Served in various Leadership roles in his previous tenure as Professor/ HoD/ Dean – Student Affairs/ Dean of Mechanical Engineering Sciences / Dean of Institutions at NHCE Bengaluru. He is an Author of Four Books and published 64 Journal / Conference Papers. He has also undertaken Five R&D Projects (AICTE-2/ VGST-1/ IEI -2), a total value of approximately INR 50 Lakhs.


Dr. Ganesha Prasad & Team has filed 32 Patent applications as of 30th June 2021, of which 29 are Patents published in the Official Indian Patent Journal, and 11 Patents commercialized. Dr. M S Ganesha Prasad in his previous tenure also was responsible for the development of a Hybrid Electric Grid (Wind & Solar) for the Institution with a capacity of 30 KWatts; Responsible for initiating and developing Electric/ solar-powered cars for the International Competition 0 shell Eco-Marathon. He has also visited multiple Universities/ Countries in connection with the R&D activities of the Institute. He has also been an Author in different Digital Magazine Articles.
